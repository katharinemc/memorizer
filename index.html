Memorizer

Memorize some text, word by word, line by line.

Memorizer helps you remember passages of text by focusing your attention on small chunks of the text. The first word. Then the first word and the second word. Then the first word, the second word, and the third - and so on. Reading these results aloud is essential to the memorization process. When you reach the end, Memorizer returns to the beginning for more practice.

Memorizer is a KatharineMc project.
